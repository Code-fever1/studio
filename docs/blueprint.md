# **App Name**: Syed Alijah

## Core Features:

- About Me Section: A dedicated space showcasing your photo and an engaging bio.
- Project Gallery: A curated collection of your work with in-depth descriptions and direct links.
- Skills Showcase: An organized section highlighting your proficiencies and expertise.
- Contact Section: An easily accessible area with social media links and contact details.

## Style Guidelines:

- Dark mode background: A dark grey (#222222) to provide contrast and sleekness.
- Primary color: Teal (#008080) for vibrant accents and highlights.
- Accent color: Light teal (#B0E0E6) to complement primary teal and create contrast.
- Font: 'Inter', a grotesque-style sans-serif, for a modern and neutral look in headlines and body text.
- Simple, modern icons that integrate seamlessly with the dark theme.
- Subtle transitions and hover effects to enhance user experience.
- Clean, minimalist layout with plenty of whitespace to ensure readability and focus on content.
